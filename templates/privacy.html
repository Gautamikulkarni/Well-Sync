Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Well-Sync ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our medical information platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details when you register
  • Health Information: Medical queries and related information you voluntarily provide
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve user experience
  • Respond to your medical queries
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Medical Information Disclaimer

All health-related information you provide is treated with strict confidentiality. However, Well-Sync is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request copies of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@well-sync.com.
